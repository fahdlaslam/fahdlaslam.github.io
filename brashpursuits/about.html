About Brash Pursuits

Welcome to Brash Pursuits. I’m head writer, CEO, and general savant-in-charge: Timothy French. I’ve been an actor in New York City for the past four or five years. In that time, I’ve had some genuinely great experiences with some genuinely great people. I’ve also, frankly, encountered a lot of bullshit. I started this website because I wanted to provide actors with a clear space to pursue experiences that will be of paramount usefulness to them. Anything I write about or endorse here will be something I’ve genuinely experienced and grown from myself, not some schlocky class, subscription service, or $39.99 motivational webisode I’m being paid to sell you. I’ve hung out in acting circles for a while, and I’ve had people convince me to pay for things I didn’t desperately need (trust me, I went to Conservatory) and that’s something I’m no longer interested in. So from here on out, I want to start sharing the worthwhile, actionable experiences I’ve had with you, so that you can save your time & energy for the thing that matters most: Acting.

- Timothy French
