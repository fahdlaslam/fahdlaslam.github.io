Are You Going To ESPAFest?

In this world, there are very few perfect places. If you read my previous post about ESPA, you know I believe at least one exists. 

I’m stoked to announce that if you live in New York City and you’re still on the fence about going to ESPA, a week full of free classes and events is just around the corner, including a free Alexander Workshop with Karen Braga, one of my favorite people of all time. 

You can get all the details over on the ESPA official website, and I highly encourage you to take part. If nothing else, you can come see me fall flat on my face in another gorgeous night of one act plays at DETENTION #32. 

If you have any questions about the ESPA experience, feel free to email me at tim@brashpursuits.com, although I honestly encourage you to swing in and see what things are like for yourself.
