We facilitate medical travel to the UK for Pakistani patients. We will walk clients through every step of the process, connecting you to medical experts in the UK for treatment. Based out of offices in London and Faisalabad.

Services

We provide a simple five-step process which we use in order to provide an unmatched service and experience for our clients, assisting them the entire way of the process- from the intial consultation to visa application processes, and optional extras including accomodation and catering services.

1. Consultation

Consultation at our office to understand your medical condition and requirements

2. Connection

Connect you to a suitable doctor in the UK to seek a medical assessment

3. Treatments

Identify treatment options and cost

4. Book

Book treatment

5. Visa

Provide full support and guidance for your visa application

Accomodation

Book accommodation in the UK (Optional)

Catering

Book caterer in the UK to provide home-cooked halal food (Optional)
