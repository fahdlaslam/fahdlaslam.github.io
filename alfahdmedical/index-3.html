We facilitate medical travel to the UK for Pakistani patients. We walk clients through every step of the process, connecting you to medical experts in the UK for treatment.

Based out of London and Faisalabad.

Services

We provide a simple five-step process which we use in order to provide an unmatched service and experience for our clients, assisting them through the entire the process from the intial consultation to the visa application process, as well as offering optional such as accommodation and catering arrangements.

STEP 1. CONSULTATION

Consultation at our office to understand your medical condition and requirements

STEP 2: CONNECTION

Connect you to a suitable doctor in the UK to seek a medical assessment

STEP 3: TREATMENTS

Identify treatment options and cost

STEP 4: BOOK

Book treatment

STEP 5: VISA

Provide full support and guidance
for your visa application
