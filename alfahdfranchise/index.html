What are we offering?

An Established Brand

Franchise opportunities provide a measure of security that is often missing from independent ventures. Purchasing a franchise allows the new owner to leverage the success of an existing business to sell a proven product. Consumers choose established brands 2 to 1 over unknown brands. Franchises with recognition increase revenue and shorten sales cycles. Since its founding in 2013, Al Fahd has established itself as a force to be reckoned with in Faisalabad.

A Proven Business Model

90% of franchised businesses are still operating after 10 years while 82% of independent businesses fail. Why? Because their business models work. Over time, franchisers have encountered and overcome most of the problems independent business owners face. Our success is proven and time-tested.

Ongoing Support

Al Fahd provides detailed upfront training on how to successfully operate your gym. We also provide continued support and advice in the areas of marketing, maintenance, customer service and human resources (including training of new staff). Our ongoing assistance is designed to strengthen business success for the franchisee. As new owners come across issues and challenges, they have access to continuous support from Al Fahd HQ.
